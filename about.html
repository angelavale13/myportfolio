About

My name is Angela Alexandra Valenzuela. Primarily a front-end web developer, my interests are also tied to UX research. A personal belief of mine is that iterating and improving on past works can be rewarding because we are constantly challenging what we think we knew yesterday.

Knowing what it's like to walk through a workflow with a client in Spanish is valuable, and I hope to take advantage of the humbling experience. Environments in which I was surrounded by seasoned associates meant that I have had to adapt on the flyand because of this it's always been important for me to be open to critical feedback.
